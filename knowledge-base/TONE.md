## The Anti-Slop Checklist
Before publishing any content, check:
1. Does it start with the right hook for this platform?
2. Are there specific numbers and examples, not vague claims?
3. Would I actually say this? (Read it aloud if unsure.)
4. Is there a clear point of view, not just neutral reporting?
5. [Any specific patterns you want to ban — em dashes, hedging, AI slop
   phrases like "in today's rapidly evolving landscape"]
6. Does it sound like a LinkedIn influencer? If yes, rewrite.
